About Us

The Hub is a an interactive tool designed to help the City of Burlington utilize their hockey arenas, halls, and community rooms, by booking more events more often. A simple 4-step process that can be done in as little as 5 minutes.

Our Mission

To make booking facilities easy for families in the City of Burlington and bring more people together more often.
